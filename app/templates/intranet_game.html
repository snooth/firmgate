How to play

Move with arrow keys or A / D. Fire with Space. Destroy all invaders to advance — each wave adds more invaders, faster movement, and heavier fire.

Use the Speed buttons (1×, 2×, 3×) to run the game faster without changing difficulty.

Retro sound effects play during gameplay — use the speaker button to mute.

Shoot the red UFO for bonus points. P pauses · R restarts.
